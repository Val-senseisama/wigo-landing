About Us

wiGO MARKET is a campus-based marketplace built for students, by students. We connect buyers, vendors, and riders in one trusted platform that makes shopping, selling, and delivery seamless within universities.

Our Mission

to empower student entrepreneurs, make campus shopping safer and faster, and create earning opportunities for riders.

Sell Smarter, Earn Faster — Right on Campus

How It Works

List Your Products

Upload product photos, prices, and descriptions. Put your store in front of the campus community.

Get Paid Securely

Earnings go straight into your wallet. Withdraw anytime to your bank account.

Sign up and Verify

Create your vendor account with Student ID or business details. Verified sellers build instant trust.

Get Orders & Deliver

Receive instant notifications when students or Customers buy. Hand over to a rider for fast, reliable delivery.
